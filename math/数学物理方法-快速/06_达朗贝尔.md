达朗贝尔公式

考虑一维波动
$$
(\frac{\part ^2}{\part t^2}-a^2\frac{\part ^2}{\part t^2})u=0
\to
(\frac{\part }{\part t} +a\frac{\part }{\part t})(\frac{\part }{\part t}-a\frac{\part }{\part t})u=0
$$
步骤2
$$
令
x=a(\xi+\eta),t=\xi-\eta
\\
由于: \frac{\part}{\part \xi}=\frac{\part t}{\part \xi}\frac{\part}{\part t} + \frac{\part x}{\part \xi}\frac{\part}{\part x}
\\
有
\frac{\part}{\part \xi} = \frac{\part}{\part t}+a \frac{\part}{\part x}，\frac{\part}{\part \eta}=-(\frac{\part}{\part t} -a \frac{\part}{\part x} )
$$
 代入原方程
$$
\frac{\part^2 u}{\part\xi\part\eta}=0
\\
对\eta积分：\frac{\part u}{\part\xi}=f(\xi)
\\
对\xi积分：\frac{\part u}{\part\eta}=f(\eta)
\\
故 u=\int f(\xi)d\xi+\int f(\eta)d\eta=g_1(x+at)+g_2(x-at)
$$
考虑无边界条件
$$
u|_{t=0}=\phi(x),u_t|_{t=0}=\psi(x)
\\代入原式\\
\phi(x)=g_1( x)+g_2(x )\\
\psi(x)=a g'_1( x)-a g'_2(x)
\\积分\\
g_1( x)-g_2(x ) = \frac1a\int^x_{x_0}\psi(\xi)d\xi+g_1(x_0)-g_2(x_0)
\\故\\
g_1(x)=\frac12\phi(x)+\frac1{2a}\int^x_{x_0}\psi(\xi)d\xi+\frac12[g_1(x_0)-g_2(x_0)]
\\
g_2(x)=\frac12\phi(x)-\frac1{2a}\int^x_{x_0}\psi(\xi)d\xi-\frac12[g_1(x_0)-g_2(x_0)]
\\代回通解\\
u(x,t)=\frac12[\phi(x+at)+\phi(x-at)]+\frac1{2a}\int^{x+at}_{x-at}\psi(\xi)d\xi
$$


得到无限长弦自由振动的达朗贝尔公式
$$
u(x,t)=\frac12[\phi(x+at)+\phi(x-at)]+\frac1{2a}\int^{x+at}_{x-at}\psi(\xi)d\xi
$$












步骤2的注：特征变换
$$
u^2_{tt} = a^2u^2_{xx} \\其特征方程为：(dx)^2-a^2(dt)^2=0\\积分曲线为:x\pm at=const\\故有特征变换：\xi(x,t),\eta(x,t) \to x=a(\xi+\eta),t=\xi-\eta
$$
